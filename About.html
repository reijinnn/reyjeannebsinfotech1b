REIJIN

Reijin is a safe haven for individuals with a deep love for art. Ray Jeanne, the founder, has been captivated by the world of artistic expression, initially sparked by the mesmerizing paintings of Amorsolo. This early exposure ignited a flame within her. She sees art as more than just images on paper or canvas; it's a means of expression, a way to communicate ideas, emotions, and perspectives to the world.


The Reijin stands as a beacon of creativity and culture, inviting visitors into a world of aesthetic wonder. Its sleek, modern facade hints at the treasures within, drawing art enthusiasts and curious wanderers alike.


We offer visitors the opportunity to immerse themselves in the world of art, stimulating intellectual curiosity and emotional response. A house for preserving and archiving cultural heritage of both historical and contemporary artworks.
